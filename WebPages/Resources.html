National Suicide Prevention Line

The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline is a suicide prevention network that provides 24/7 services for anyone in suicidal crisis or emotional distress. This number can be reached at 1-800-273-8255

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMSHA) National Helpline

SAMSHA's Helpline is a 24 hour, seven days a week, confidential information service for individuals or family members facing mental or substance use disorders. This helpline can be reached at 1-800-662-HELP (4357) They also have a Treatment Referral Routing Service that can be reached at 1-800-487-4889.

National Insitute of Mental Health (NIMH)

The National Insitute of Mental Health is a leading federal agency for research on mental disorders. This agency has extensive information on mental disorders and ways to educate and raise awareness as well as information to seek help. Their website can be found at: https://www.nimh.nih.gov/index.shtml

National Alliance on Mental Health (NAMI) Helpline

The NAMI Helpline is a free nationwide support service that provides information, resource referrals, and support for those who have mental conditions. This helpline can be reached at 1-800-950-NAMI (6264) Monday through Friday from 10AM to 6PM, ET
